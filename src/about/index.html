Blumstudio is an office offering architectural, urban planning, interior design and design services. We strive to create high quality architecture, trying not to undergo temporary mods. We design modern and functional architecture, embedded in the context of place and economic realities. It is important for us to have a close relationship with the investor, which makes it easier for us to meet the client's ideas, needs and dreams.

Architecture: urban plots analysis, building inventories, conceptual projects, full architectural and building documentation of buildings (single family and multi-family, service and office buildings) together with land development and industry projects, adaptation of existing projects, extension and modernization of existing buildings, Official documentation, author's supervision

Interiors: individual projects of private and commercial interiors, conceptual designs, 3d visualizations, full executive documentation, selection of finishing and interior furnishings, lighting projects, individual projects of furniture, consultancy and consultancy

Graphics: visualizations, usable graphics, corporate visual identity, visual information system of objects, graphic design of mobile applications

The owner of the office is Kamila Jacyniuk - a graduate of the Faculty of Architecture and Urban Planning at the Wrocław University of Technology (2009), studied at the École Nationale Supérieure d'Architecture de Nantes in France in 1 year Erasmus Scholarschip. She has gained professional experience since 2008 in a renowned architectural studio in Wroclaw. Since 2010 she runs her own design studio. Member of the Lower Silesian Chamber of Architects - Building Permits in Architectural Design for Unrestricted Design No. 04 / DSOKK / 2014. She also completed Advertising Studies in Wrocław, gaining experience in graphic design.
