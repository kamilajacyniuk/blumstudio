Reconstruction of the old bar BARBARA for European Capital of Culture 2016

location: ul. Świdnicka, Wrocław

area: 1348 m2

project date: 2013

realisation date: 2016



The general concept for interior design of the ECC 2016 Festival's office building was to create a neutral space that would create background for artistic events on the occasion of the European Capital of Culture Wrocław 2016. For this purpose the colors of the interior are monochrome. White interior are perfect for the exhibition function. In the ground floor level a terrazzo floor is laid in white with white-gray tiles. Walls plastered and painted. In the office space floor floors finished with carpeting in light gray color. Throughout the monochrome space of the building stand the pillars with the original mosaic reproduced ceramic in black and white. Minimal lighting in the form of busbars and linear lighting profiles. In space multifunctional hall uses truss grates for professional lighting and sound equipment.
